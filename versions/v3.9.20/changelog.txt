# Prominence II Hasturian Era
v3.9.20 Guns and Demons

A **new subclass** just dropped! Guns are now an early to mid game alternative to Bows and crossbows. These Guns scale with ranged damage, but need a ton of ammo! Here is the first four horsemen!

- Gatling gun: 20 second spin time, decent dps but it will slow you down and is a bit inaccurate
- Hunter Cannon: Big boom! This weapon has the highest single target Damage out of them all, with a large cooldown!
- Hunter Blunderbuss: A general jack of all trades, shoots 3 projectiles each doing decent damage and having the highest Velocity out of them all, not the most accurate
- Hunter Pistol: The Blunderbuss' smaller brother is great for anyone who likes to shoot fast and having something to do whilst the other guys are reloading

All of these work **with Infinity**, so dont worry about Ammo later on!
*Why would i care about these, im a Warrior!!?*
These guns **aren't only for Ranged Classes**! Guns come with unique enchants that can compliment **other playstyles!**

- Tether Pulls an opponent(or friend if you fell silly) closer to you, just not all the way
- Chain Lightning gives the guns a smaller AOE effect that can take care of large masses.

Do note that their recipes have been adjusted, they are harder and require a Lord soul to craft.

## Changes
- Tweaked Ritual of Insanity as it was broken and confused players, sometimes sticking around for longer than it should, it will now give misfortune
- Hotfixed & Lowered all Bewitchment seeds dropping from grass and such
- Extremely lowered the amount of Dragons Blood Saplings can spawn in the Nether Fortress chests
- Higher chance of Baphomet dropping more loot if Looting is used
- Optimized Hellhounds' drop to drop more loot when killed with looting 
- Upped Snakes drop more loot when killed with Looting
- Reworked Vampires' drops. They drop more Blood bottles when killed with looting and in general
- Ironed out Ghosts drops to drop less Ectoplasm
- Significantly increased Demons' drops. Dropping more Demon horns and Demon hearts (Demons don't adhere to human anatomy!)
- Now Snakes from Naturalist are added in the Pool of snakes that can drop Snake tongues, keep in mind Rattlesnakes and coal snakes do not do this yet!
- Obviously, we didn't forget about cosmetics! You can now Transmog your Icarus Wings and your Travelers Backpacks!
- Thankfully you no longer unequip Trinkets when you scroll over them!
- Yep. Disabled the Mutant Skeleton Skull's Ability. It was crazy op lol
- Expanded Crossbows compatibility with Mutant Skeleton Armor.
- The guns may be worse than Orion as of now, but will be improved in the future.
