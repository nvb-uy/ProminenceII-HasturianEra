# Prominence II: Hasturian Era
**3.9.19 - It's been 84 years...**
This Update fixes some Energizing Orb Recipes and removes Lignite from findable ores as its just a weird looking coal that serves no purpose other than confusing players. All Lignite Coal in your World will be replaced with normal coal or Deepslate coal.

Also brings quite a lot of updates to some mods, improving performance for all. It also rolls out a couple of smaller changes to Warriors and Rogues early game, making them scale more and be slightly less strong early on.

## Changes
- Added more recipes to the Energizing Orb
- Added a Paladin Questline in the Gear and combat Tab - the entire tab will most likely get a overhaul in the upcoming weeks
- You can now void Mana
- Added Savanna and snowy Chef's delight Structures
- Added config option to mute whole mods
- Added two new ruins
- Following upgrades can be used when backpack is placed as a block: Magnet, Pickup, Feeding, Refill, Furnace, Smoker and Blast Furnace Upgrades
- Added more meaningful quest rewards to the Gear and Combat quest tab early on
- Fixed a ton of Energizing Orb recipes not working, oops(Thank you d9ze(pq))
- Fixed Blackstone Golem Ritual Quest requiring the wrong boss Quest done
- Fixed Trinkets causing your inventory Player to flicker
- Removed the Archon combustion charge Questline as it is no longer completable
- Removed a lot of Obsolete Quests from the Spellcasting Questline that are either explained in "Unlocking Abilities" or the "Combat & Gear" Questlines
- Removed Lignite from spawning, as it is just a differently looking coal with no other use, lowers the total amount of ores confusing players - note that this will not remove any lignite from chests, machines or recipes, it can still be obtained in the Quarry, for now

## Balance Changes
- Warrior Set: +4% > +3% physical damage
- Berserker Set: 3, 8, 6, 3 > 2, 6, 5, 2 armor, +5% > +4% physical damage +10% > +5% knockback resistance
- Netherite berserker: 0 > 1, 1, 1, 1 armor toughness
- Rogue Armor: 5% > 2% speed, 4% > 3% haste
- Assassin Set: 5% > 4% speed, 5% > 4% haste
- Netherite Assassin: 5% > 4% physical damage, Chestplate specifically 4 > 5 armor, 0 > 1, 1, 1, 1 toughness
